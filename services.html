Services

At Woodland Resources Unlimited difficult tree removal is our specialty, but we also offer many other services.

Contact us today for an estimate!

Tree Removal

Our tree removal services will extract sick or dead trees with our trained climber. To remove your tree, our tree care technician will go to the top of the tree and rope limb by limb to ensure safe removal. Once the tree is down, you can request stump removal services to grind the stump down.

Small Acreage And Lot Clearing

Looking to clear a lot, so you can begin a new project? We offer small acreage and lot clearing to remove trees, shrubery, etc. to leave you with an open plot of land.

Tree Pruning

Our tree trimming services will remove dead and overgrown branches on all sizes of trees. These branches will be trimmed away from your home in such a way to promote healthy growth in order to keep your trees healthy and looking great.

Tree Planting

Planting a tree can be a big process. We are here to help you plant trees, so they can thrive.

Debris Removal/Stump Grinding

After a tree is removed we offer stump grinding to completly clear the tree. We also offer the removal of all debris from the tree trimming or removal.

Disease Prevention And Treatment

Have you noticed weak, diseased, or damaged limbs on your trees? If so, your tree may have internal damage or it might be facing a combination of stress factors including insects, bugs, or diseases. If a problem is discovered, a licensed pesticide applicator is available to treat your trees for disease or insect damage. In addition, together we will work on preventative measures to keep your yard healthy in the future. We also offer disease prevention services.
