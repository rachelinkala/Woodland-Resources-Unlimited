Services

At Woodland Resources Unlimited difficult tree removal is our specialty, but we also offer many other services.

Contact us today for an estimate!

Tree Removal

Our tree removal services will extract sick or dead trees with our trained climber. To remove your tree, we will go to the top of the tree and rope limb by limb to ensure safe removal.

Small Acreage And Lot Clearing

Looking to clear a lot for a project or animal habitat? We offer minimal impact clearing to lots and small acreage parcels.

Tree Pruning

Our pruning services focus on the overall health and appearance of your trees. Overgrown, dead, and structurally unsound branches are removed with proper cutting techniques. This insures that the tree can properly heal itself upon the completion of pruning.

Stump Grinding

We offer stump grinding with every tree removal. Or, if you have any unwanted stumps in your yard we can take care of that too.

Debris Removal and Storm Damage

Dealing with the aftermath of a storm or high winds can be an extremely overwhelming process. We are here to help you get your property cleaned up and back in order.

Disease Prevention And Treatment

Have you noticed weak, diseased, or damaged trees on your property? If so, your trees may be facing a combination of stress factors from insects or diseases. If a problem is discovered, we can look at options for treatment, removal, and/or prevention.
